The Project

If you've ever been to the IFI gift shop you'll know how full to the brim it is with movies from all over the world. Awesome, right? But it can also trigger decision paralysis from choice overload. Now, do you remember those paper fortune tellers that were really popular in the 90s? Well, a while ago, to entertain ourselves, we made one of those to help us decide what foreign language film to select next from the IFI gift shop. This fortune teller decided what country of origin we would select our next movie from. Genius, we agree. Also a good excuse to have a coffee and slice of cake while choosing. This is all a long winded way of explaining where the inspiration for this app came from. Another way to select foreign language films, but with the added bonus of taking into account the IMDB and Rotten Tomatoes ratings. It's a work in progress & feedback is welcome, but for now - hope you enjoy!
